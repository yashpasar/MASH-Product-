Why MASH Features RSS

ROM Features

MASH is a custom ROM built on top of the Android stack, with CyanogenMod as the base.

Configurations

MASH comes with all the necessary configurations baked in. It has just the right settings, for you to tinker with.

Latest

MASH promises latest updates to your Android device, without any compromise in the integrity of the stock OS.

Themes

It comes with CyanogenMod Theme Engine out of the box. Theme your device, just the way you love it.

Open Source

We love open source, and we love the community that drives it. Hence, MASH has its blocks readily available at github.

Home like

Android is close to our hearts, and it is equally close to MASH. We promised you a home like UX, and we did it.

Community Support

Found a bug? Didn't love the Easter egg? The vast developer base will make sure it's fixed. Just let us know!
